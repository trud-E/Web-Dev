Travel to Norway

Do you want to see real Vikings?

If this headline interests you, then you need to visit Norway. Surely, you yourself have heard a lot about Norway - about the country of fjords and Vikings, where from a mixture of water in all its physical states, rocks, greenery and a very special atmosphere, an amazing world emerges that attracts you with its unusualness.Fjords and ferries are a big part of the most popular excursions. People come here to look at the rocks from the water, sail through all the fjords, visit the Lofoten Islands, learn more about the harsh life of the Vikings and go fishing.

We look at Norway from a different perspective. Active holidays with us include trekking, excursions to off-the-beaten-path places and new places that are unlike anything else. Preacher's Rock, Pea, Troll's Tongue, Folgefonna glacier, Mount Galhepiggen, Bessegen trail - we see all this with our own eyes and get to know the very soul of Norway!
