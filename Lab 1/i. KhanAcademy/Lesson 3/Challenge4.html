Nuggets like McDonald's

  • Time: 1 hours
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken fillet 300g
Chicken egg 2pcs
White loaf 100g
Salt and pepper to taste
Breadcrumbs 150g
Vegetable oil for frying

Step 1: If the fillet is frozen, defrost and dry it.
Step 2: Cut the loaf into cubes, place in a blender, and grind into crumbs.
Step 3: Next, cut the chicken fillet and blend it into a smooth mince.
Step 4: Pour bread crumbs into a deep bowl, add minced chicken.
Step 5: Add salt and pepper to taste, beat in a small egg.
Step 6: Knead the minced meat thoroughly until smooth.
Step 7: Line a cutting board with foil, use wet hands to form small pieces in the shape of classic nuggets, place them on the foil, and put them in the freezer for 15-20 minutes.
Step 8: Meanwhile, beat the remaining egg until lightly foamy. Dip the nuggets in it.
Step 9: Roll them in breadcrumbs.
Step 10: And fry in a small amount of vegetable oil until golden brown. Cooking time will depend on the height of your blanks, but you should not overdry them.

Banana pancakes

  • Time: 20 minute
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 320ml
Banana 1pcs
Baking powder 2 teaspoons
Chicken Egg 1pcs
Salt pinch
Sugar 1 tablespoon
Butter 30g
Wheat flour 1.5 cups

Step 1: To prepare, take the products from the list. You need a large banana. And the longer it has been lying, the better. Ideally, if its peel is very dark.
Step 2: Mash the banana with a fork, melt the butter, and mix the eggs with sugar and salt.
Step 3: Then combine all three mixtures with milk and stir.
Step 4: Add flour mixed with baking powder.
Step 5: Mix everything well so that there are no lumps. The dough will be like pancake dough, maybe a little thicker.
Step 6: Place the dough on a dry, hot frying pan. When bubbles appear on the surface, turn the pancake over and fry until done.

Caprese Salad

  • Time: 5 minute
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Mozzarella 2 balls
Tomatoes 2-3pcs
Basil 0.5-1 bunch (to taste)
Ground black pepper to taste
Olive oil to taste
Balsamic vinegar as desired
Salt to taste

Step 1: Cut the mozzarella into slices.
Step 2: Also cut the tomatoes into not too thick circles.
Step 3: Place the tomatoes and cheese on a plate, alternating the pieces. Sprinkle with salt and ground black pepper. Salt - ideally coarse sea salt, freshly ground pepper.
Step 4: Salad dressing is a matter of taste, there are many options. The simplest option is to sprinkle the salad with olive oil. No less popular is a dressing of olive oil and balsamic vinegar - oil and vinegar can be pre-mixed or simply poured over the salad one by one.
Step 5: Add basil leaves and serve the salad.
